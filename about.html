Our Story

Bright Horizons began with a simple belief: every child deserves a soft place to land with learning.

Bright Horizons Tutoring was created for students who need more than just extra worksheets. We exist for learners who benefit from patient explanations, positive encouragement, and a space where it’s okay to ask, “Can you show me that one more time?”

Founder Spotlight

Meet Sophie Fiza

Bright Horizons was founded by Sophie Fiza, who was a part of a passionate group of educators that believe every child deserves the chance to reach their full academic potential. She started Bright Horizons as a small after school program in Richmond, Virginia and expanded for maximum progress for students all over!

Sophie’s vision is simple: create tutoring spaces where students feel safe to try, supported when they struggle, and proud when they grow. Her goal is to make learning feel lighter, more hopeful, and truly within reach.

How we support students and families

At Bright Horizons, we focus on more than just getting through tonight’s homework. We work alongside families to understand each student’s needs in math and reading, then design a plan that fits their pace, personality, and goals.

Our sessions balance structure with encouragement: clear explanations, guided practice, and space for questions—paired with consistent check-ins so families always know how things are going. We want learners to feel seen, not rushed, and to leave each session feeling a little more confident than when they arrived.

Vision & Values

Our work is guided by compassion, clarity, and consistency. We create calm, encouraging spaces where mistakes are part of learning, not something to be ashamed of. We value honest communication, realistic goals, and steady, meaningful progress for every student.

What matters most to us

  • Students feeling capable—not overwhelmed
  • Real growth in math and reading foundations
  • Partnership with families, not one-way updates

Our Vision

Our vision is to help shape a generation of students who see challenges as opportunities, not roadblocks. We want Bright Horizons to be more than a place to “fix” grades—it should feel like a place where students rediscover what they’re capable of, one step at a time.

By blending thoughtful instruction, caring mentorship, and strong family partnerships, we hope every learner who passes through Bright Horizons walks away with skills they can use, confidence they can feel, and a belief that their future truly stretches beyond the horizon.
